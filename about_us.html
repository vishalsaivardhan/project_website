ABOUT KEC

Kuppam Engineering College (KEC) was inaugurated by the technology-savvy Hon'ble Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh Sri. N. Chandra Babu Naidu garu on 11-09-2001. The Institute was promoted by philanthropist Dr. B.C. Nagaraj, Founder Chairman of BCN Group of Institutions.

KEC is an Autonomous Institution accredited by NBA & NAAC, certified by ISO 9001:2015, and recognized by UGC under 2(f) & 12(b). It is graded 'A' by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Under the leadership of Dr. N. Sunil Raj, M.B.B.S. (Vice-Chairman), Sri. N. Sagar Raj (Secretary), and Principal Dr. S. Sudhakar Babu, the institution continues to grow with innovation and excellence.
